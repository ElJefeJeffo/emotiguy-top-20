#21 Emotiguy: Default Expression

The Basic Face, But Still Great!

This version of Emotiguy isn't as expressive as the other variations on this list, but it still has its uses as an emoji.

It can be used as a reaction image to something idiotic or strange a person has said online. In a joking way of course!

Overall, this version of Emotiguy isn't bad or good. But it does serve a purpose in this day and age of the world wide web.
